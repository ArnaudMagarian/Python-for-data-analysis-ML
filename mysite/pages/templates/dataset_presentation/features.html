Description of the dataset features

  • Gender

    Person Gender. Male or Female.

  • Age

    Person Age.

  • Height

    Person Height.

  • Weight

    Person Weight.

  • family_history_with_overweight

    Is their an obesity record in the person's family. yes or no.

  • FAVC

    Frequent consumption of high caloric food. yes or no.

  • FCVC

    Frequency of consumption of vegetables. 1, 2 or 3.

  • NCP

    Number of main meals. 1, 2, 3 or 4.

  • CAEC

    Consumption of food between meals. no, Sometimes, Frequently or Always.

  • SMOKE

    Is the person smoking. yes or no.

  • CH2O

    Consumption of water daily. 1, 2 or 3.

  • CALC

    Consumption of alcohol. no, Sometimes, Frequently or Always.

  • SCC

    Calories consumption monitoring. yes or no.

  • FAF

    Physical activity frequency. 0, 1, 2 or 3.

  • TUE

    Time using technology devices. 0, 1 or 2.

  • MTRANS

    Mean of transportation used. Automobile, Public_Transportation or Walking.
